Origin of Chess

  • The history of chess can be traced back nearly 1,500 years to its earliest known predecessor, called chaturanga, in India.
  • It spread to Persia, where it was modified in terms of shapes and rules and developed into Shatranj.
  • The game evolved into its current form by about 1500 CE.
  • "Romantic chess" was the predominant playing style from the late 18th century to the 1880s.
